# Nature's Elixir - Shipping & Fulfillment

## Shipping Requirements

### Product-Specific Shipping Considerations

#### Tea

- **Packaging**: Lightweight, may require protective packaging
- **Fragility**: Moderate - requires protection from moisture and light
- **Shipping Profile**: Standard shipping profile

#### Spartan Oils

- **Packaging**: May require special handling for glass containers
- **Fragility**: High - glass bottles require protective packaging
- **Shipping Profile**: Fragile items shipping profile
- **Regulations**: May have restrictions for international shipping

#### Natural Sponges

- **Packaging**: Natural products, may require breathable packaging
- **Fragility**: Low to moderate
- **Shipping Profile**: Standard shipping profile

## Shipping Methods

### Domestic Shipping (Ireland)

**Standard Shipping**

- Price: €5.00
- Estimated delivery: 3-5 business days
- Carrier options: An Post, DHL, etc.

**Express Shipping**

- Price: €10.00
- Estimated delivery: 1-2 business days
- Carrier options: An Post, DHL, etc.

**Free Shipping**

- Price: Free (for orders over €45.00)
- Condition: Order subtotal ≥ €45.00
- Estimated delivery: Same as standard shipping
- Carrier options: An Post, DHL, etc.

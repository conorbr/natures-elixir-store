## Business Description

Nature's Elixir is a small-scale e-commerce business specializing in natural and wellness products. Jim operates from his home garage in Dublin, Ireland, and primarily sells at weekend markets throughout Dublin. The online store serves as a supplementary sales channel, allowing market customers to learn about and purchase products online.

**Key Business Characteristics**:

- Small-scale, local business
- Primary sales channel: Weekend markets in Dublin
- Fulfillment: Home garage in Dublin
- Online store purpose: Simple online presence to direct market customers to online ordering

## Product Categories

Nature's Elixir offers three main product categories:

1. **Tea** - Various types of natural and organic teas
2. **Spartan Oils** - Natural oils and essential oil products
3. **Natural Sponges** - Organic and natural sponge products
